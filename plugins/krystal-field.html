KrystalField

Overview

KrystalField is a mastering-grade dynamic processor featuring dual compression engines for simultaneous downward and upward compression. This approach provides precise control over mix dynamics, making it suitable for mastering, bus processing, and final mix polish.

Features

  • Dual dynamic processors for downward and upward compression
  • Target-based threshold control
  • Unified attack and release controls
  • Independent ratio and knee controls
  • RMS, Peak, and LOUD detection modes (ITU BS.1770)
  • Auto makeup gain
  • Mix control for parallel processing
  • Lookahead delay (0-20ms)
  • Low frequency bias for sidechain filtering
  • Hard and soft clipping modes
  • Bypass for A/B comparison
  • Gain reduction and short-term LUFS metering
  • Real-time waveform display
  • Low latency and CPU efficient
